HER (POEM)

Since first laying eyes upon your grace,

I've thanked god for the creation- that's you.

Though heaven's location remains unknown,

In your presence, it's glimpses I've known.

I don't know what is this journey's span,

I can't fathom how far we'll go, hand in hand.

But one thing is certain, my love, it's true,

I'll never let you wander, no matter what we go through.

Even in my darkest days,

You are the beacon of light, the radiant bliss I've seen many fall, fall in love with you

They may adore you, but not like I do

My heart's devotion is solely for you.

This moment I've longed for, it's finally here, Hold me close, my love, draw me near.

For in your embrace, I've found all I sought, Something I never thought now I've got,

With your love I'm complete, in your arms, I feel home,

For you my darling the love is eternal and profound.

LOVE

I've come to believe that we are forever bound to ones,

that we share blood with, but still I see,

A war in your eyes against your blood for me.

You say I don't express enough but only if You saw me glare at you when you spin in that dress,

Or thank go for this indwelling bliss.

My love for you is beyond reason or promise,

beyond peace or happiness and I would still love you

Against all the discouragement of this world.

They ask me to give up cause they fail to fathom my love,

For me the possibility of you is better than the reality of others.

And this shall be my expression of love,

You might not be at the end of my story,

But, even in oblivion you shall be in my memory.
